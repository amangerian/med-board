Cards imported from Anki refer to their pictures by filename (e.g. paste-a1b2c3.jpg). If the pictures didn't travel with the cards, those images can't display. Add them here once and they'll appear on every card that references them.

Manual entry
For scanned reports the app can't read. Enter an exam label, then type each area's standard score after the colon (optionally score, peer-mean). Blank lines and areas you skip are ignored. Without a peer mean, weakness is judged against the national average of ~500.

Scheduling algorithm

Target retention (%)
FSRS schedules each card to come back when recall drops to this level. Higher = more reviews. 90% is the standard default; 85% for lighter workload, 93-95% close to exam day.

Backup & Restore

Your cards and progress live only in this browser. Back up regularly — a backup file lets you restore everything or move to another computer/browser.
Full backup
Download everything — cards, scheduling, streak & aviary, settings, and your parsed ITE / MKSAP / PRITE score reports — as a .json file
Restore from backup
Merge keeps your existing cards and their progress, adds any cards the backup has that you don't, and updates your ITE/MKSAP/PRITE reports from the file. Replace wipes this device and restores the backup exactly — use this when setting up a new phone or browser.

About

Content areas sourced from the official ABIM Internal Medicine Certification Exam Blueprint (January 2026) and ABPN Psychiatry Certification Content Specifications (September 2025). Spaced repetition uses FSRS-5 (default parameters trained on ~700M Anki reviews) with classic SM-2 selectable above. Cards reviewed under SM-2 migrate automatically on their next review — no progress is lost when switching.
